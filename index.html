Born in Blue Ridge, Georgia

Ridge Runner Gear grew out of Appalachian roots, mechanical design experience, and years of asking the same question around camp: “Wouldn’t it be nice if…”

After retiring from a career in mechanical design engineering, I began turning trail ideas into real gear — products designed to solve problems, carry efficiently, and hold up to real use outdoors.

Around here, ridge runners are known for knowing the hills, solving problems with what they have, and making things that work. That spirit lives in every Ridge Runner design.

The Ridge Runner Gear Philosophy

Outdoor gear should do more, carry less, and last longer. Every Ridge Runner product is built around thoughtful engineering, practical field use, and the belief that good gear should be worth carrying.

Do More With Less

Multi-use gear means fewer items to carry, fewer parts to fail, and fewer problems to solve once you are out on the trail.

Designed With Engineering

Every product begins as a detailed CAD model so each part has a purpose before it ever reaches the workbench or the outdoors.

Built For Real Use

Ridge Runner Gear is tested in the field, refined at the bench, and designed by someone who actually wants the gear to work when it matters.
