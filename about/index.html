Our Story

Ridge Runner Gear began in the mountains of Blue Ridge, Georgia, at the southern edge of the Appalachian range.

Around there, locals joke that mountain people are “ridge runners” with one leg longer than the other from walking hillsides their whole lives. Growing up in those mountains builds a deep appreciation for the outdoors and the gear you rely on when you’re far from the trailhead.

After retiring from a career as a mechanical design engineer, I began spending more time doing something I’ve always loved: luxury base-camp backpacking.

I enjoy the wilderness, but I also appreciate a few creature comforts while “roughing it.”

On many trips with friends, we kept saying the same thing:

“Wouldn’t it be nice if…”

Wouldn’t it be nice if one piece of gear could do two jobs?
Wouldn’t it be nice if we could carry less but still have everything we need?

That idea became Ridge Runner Gear.

Our mission is simple: design rugged outdoor tools that do more, weigh less, and last longer.

We wanted gear with more than one purpose so we could carry less without giving up comfort or capability. We also wanted gear that could be refurbished to extend its life, reduce waste, and be more cost-effective over time.

Every product is designed, manufactured, and assembled by hand. Whenever possible, we use American-sourced materials and components.

Our gear is built using materials chosen specifically for outdoor durability:

  • ASA for UV resistance
  • TPU for flexibility and impact resistance
  • Stainless steel for corrosion-resistant strength

Ridge Runner Gear is still a very small company — right now it’s just me.

And that’s intentional. It means every design solves a real problem experienced on the trail.

What We Believe

At Ridge Runner Gear, we believe you shouldn’t have to choose between comfort and capability.

Our gear is meticulously engineered for those who seek the serenity of the wilderness without sacrificing the sophistication of well-designed essentials. Whether you’re setting up a base camp beneath alpine stars or trekking toward your next breathtaking vista, our products deliver premium performance with minimalist weight.

Because luxury isn’t about excess — it’s about excellence, wherever the trail leads.

Our Values

We are a faith-based company, guided by Christian values in everything we do — from the way we design our gear to how we serve our customers.

We believe the outdoors is one of God’s greatest gifts, and we’re passionate about helping others enjoy it responsibly and respectfully. That’s why we practice Leave No Trace principles and strive to be good stewards of the wild places we love.

American Made, Trail Ready

Proudly American Made, our gear is built with rugged durability and refined utility for the serious adventurer.

If it doesn’t help make a trip lighter, simpler, more useful, or more enjoyable, it doesn’t belong in the Ridge Runner Gear lineup.
